Straight answers

Frequently asked questions

Are you a real estate agent or broker?

No. AssetLift Buyers is a direct property-buying brand of AssetLift Lending LLC. We are purchasing for our own account, not listing your property or representing you as an agent.

Will I get full market value?

A direct cash offer is generally lower than the potential retail price because it accounts for repairs, carrying costs, resale risk, and the convenience of an as-is sale. You should compare your options and choose what best fits your goals.

Do I need to make repairs or remove everything?

No. We evaluate homes in their current condition. Depending on the agreement, unwanted items may be left behind.

Can you stop a foreclosure?

We cannot stop a foreclosure or provide legal advice. In some cases, completing a sale before a lender’s deadline may help an owner avoid the property proceeding to foreclosure. Timing, payoff, title, and lender requirements control what is possible. Contact a qualified New York or New Jersey attorney and your lender promptly.

Are there fees or obligations?

There is no fee to request an offer and no obligation to accept it. AssetLift does not charge an agent commission. Your written purchase agreement and closing statement will show any transaction-specific costs or credits.
