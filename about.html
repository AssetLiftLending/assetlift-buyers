About AssetLift

A direct conversation with the people buying your property.

Meet Yisroel Pentelnik

Yisroel Pentelnik leads AssetLift Buyers, the property-acquisition brand of AssetLift Lending LLC. Over four years, the team has purchased more than 50 homes and developed a process for evaluating properties that need repairs, inherited homes, time-sensitive sales, and vacant land.

AssetLift does not list homes or act as the seller’s agent. We evaluate each opportunity for our own purchase and present an offer based on the property’s condition, location, repair needs, title, and expected resale risk.

Our approach

Be direct

Explain the process, the tradeoffs, and what still needs to be confirmed.

Respect the decision

A cash offer is one option. Owners should compare alternatives without pressure.

Follow through

Work with established closing professionals and communicate when title or timing changes.

What we will not promise

We do not promise full retail value, guaranteed five-day closings, or the ability to stop a foreclosure. Those claims ignore repairs, title, lender requirements, and legal deadlines. We promise to review the facts and communicate what appears achievable.
