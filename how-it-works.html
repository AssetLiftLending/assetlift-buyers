Simple by design

From property details to closing—without the listing process.

Our process is designed for owners who value speed, certainty, and an as-is sale.

1. Tell us about the property

Share the address, type of property, current condition, occupancy, known title concerns, and your preferred timeline. Photos help us understand repairs but are not required for an initial conversation.

2. We research the details

We review public records, nearby sales, property condition, likely repair needs, and resale risk. We may ask to visit the property or request additional documentation before making a final offer.

3. Review a clear, no-obligation offer

We explain the proposed price and timing. You are free to accept, decline, compare it with an agent’s opinion, or consult your attorney. There is no charge to request an offer.

4. Sign only if it works for you

If you accept, the terms are placed in a written purchase agreement. You should read it carefully and may have a qualified attorney review it before signing.

5. Title and closing

The closing professionals check ownership, mortgages, liens, judgments, estate authority, and other title matters. Clear-title purchases may close in as little as five days; title problems, probate, lender payoffs, or municipal requirements can take longer.

Important: An initial response within minutes is not the same as a final property offer. Closing speed depends on title, access, documentation, and agreed terms.
