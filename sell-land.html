Vacant property

Turn unwanted New York or New Jersey land into a direct sale.

Land we consider

Residential building lots, infill parcels, rural acreage, inherited land, tax-delinquent parcels, and lots without current approvals. A property does not need to be cleared or improved for us to review it.

What affects a land offer?

Zoning, legal access, road frontage, utilities, wetlands or flood areas, topography, surveys, environmental concerns, taxes, municipal liens, subdivision potential, and nearby land sales all affect value.

Helpful information

Share the street address or parcel number, county, approximate acreage, survey if available, known approvals, utility status, tax amount, and anything you know about access or wetlands.

An offer is subject to confirming parcel identity, ownership, title, access, zoning, and other due diligence.
